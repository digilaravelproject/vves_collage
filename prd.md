## 6. Data Safety & Protection (Zero-Tolerance)
- **Anti-Copy Measures**: Disable Right-click, Copy, Paste, and Text selection globally.
- **Keyboard Protection**: Disable Ctrl+S, Ctrl+U, Ctrl+C, F12.
- **Media Lockdown**: Disable image drag-and-drop; prevent "Open in new tab" for images.
- **Watermarks**: Mandatory watermark for all institutional and gallery images.
- **Technical Security**: Firewall, daily backups, and encrypted database.

---

## 7. Interlinking & User Control
- **Dynamic Interlinking**: 
    - Institution Page → Central Admissions
    - Institution Page → Respective Infrastructure Division
    - Awards → Gallery categories (Sports, Cultural, Management).
- **Access Control (RBAC)**:
    - **Admin**: Full access + 2FA.
    - **Staff**: Limited editing (Maker-Checker system).
- **Password Policy**: 8-12 chars, mandatory special characters, expiry limits.
